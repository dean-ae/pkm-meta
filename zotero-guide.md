Zotero is a reference manager.

## Extensions

Download the `.xpi` files:

- [Better BibTex](https://retorque.re/zotero-better-bibtex/) is used to export your citations into a  `.bib` file.
- [ZotMoov](https://github.com/wileyyugioh/zotmoov) is used to move files to a location.

Install the extensions `Tools > Plugins`.

- `Edit > Settings > Better BibTeX`
	- `Citation keys`
		- `Citation key formula`: (authEtal2).lower + year
	- `Automatic export`
		- `Format:` Better BibLaTeX
	- ☑️ Keep updated
	- ☑️ Background export
	- *ZoteroLibrary.bib*
